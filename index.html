Many years ago I was fan of a very popular PC games in Spain called PCFutbol v4.0. That game was about taking control of a football (soccer) club in Spanish second division and taking it to be the European Champion League. Some versions later, the user could pick an third division team. That wasn't enough for me: I wanted to play a recently created team and play from the lowest level in Spanish football, that is, 6th division which is only played by amateur players. Now, I take over and expanded dream and wish to create a multiplayer online game of footbal strategy in order to allow any user to create his own club and play against any other people in the world.

This project aims not only to create a game, but to build a community around this sport, gathering supporters, beginners and programmers in order to involve all of them to make a place to talk about our passion and polish every aspect in the play. So, everyone could be a part in the project make it of his own. At the same time, a database will created to cover every aspect of the game: historical pictures, sounds, videos and the memories of all the supporters. I'd like this project to become the largest place in the Internet where to talk about football.

FAQ

How can I get enrolled?
There are two ways: first is donating some cash, this a huge project and it'll be not compatible with a job, so this will be my job. I'll need designers and front end developers, so people who knows something about this will be welcome. PHP, Javascript are needed too.
Do you plan making a lot of money with this project?
Not really. I'm willing this place to become a referencial place in the net for all football fans. If this project comes to a happy end it'll be a dream come true. In the other hand, if some people get some cash in the meantime?. :-)
